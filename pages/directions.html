Getting Here

From Montreal

~90 km / ~1 hour
  1. Take Autoroute 15 South toward the US border
  2. Exit onto Route 202 West
  3. Follow Route 202 through Hemmingford to Havelock
  4. The fairgrounds are on Route 202 in the village

From Ottawa / Gatineau

~230 km / ~2.5 hours
  1. Take Highway 417 East to Autoroute 40 East
  2. Continue to Autoroute 30 South, then Autoroute 15 South
  3. Exit onto Route 202 West toward Hemmingford
  4. Follow Route 202 to the Havelock Fairgrounds

From New York State (USA)

~15 km from the border crossing
  1. Take I-87 North to the Champlain / Lacolle border crossing
  2. After customs, continue on Autoroute 15 North
  3. Exit onto Route 202 West toward Hemmingford
  4. Follow Route 202 to Havelock — just minutes away

From Sherbrooke

~200 km / ~2 hours
  1. Take Autoroute 10 West toward Montreal
  2. Merge onto Autoroute 35 South / Autoroute 15 South
  3. Exit onto Route 202 West toward Hemmingford
  4. Follow Route 202 to the Havelock Fairgrounds

Visitor Information

Parking

Free parking is available on the fairgrounds. Overflow parking with signage along Route 202. Follow the volunteer attendants on arrival.

Accessibility

Accessible parking near the main entrance. The fairgrounds are mostly flat and grass. Portable washrooms are available on site.

Crossing the Border?

The Havelock Fairgrounds are just minutes from the Lacolle / Champlain border crossing (I-87 / Autoroute 15). If travelling from the United States, remember to bring valid identification (passport or enhanced driver's licence). Allow extra time for customs during peak weekends.
