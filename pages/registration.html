Havelock Fair 2026 — September 12–13

What Would You Like to Register For?

The Havelock Fair welcomes competitors in four categories. Our bilingual registration form covers all classes — livestock, indoor, youth, and equestrian.

🐄

General Competition

Livestock entries (heavy horses, cattle, sheep, poultry, rabbits) and indoor classes (baking, preserves, vegetables, flowers, photography, arts & crafts, and more).

Classes 1–25
🧒

Youth (Juniors)

Open to youth competitors. Class assigned automatically by age at time of registration.

Classes 26–27
🍀

4-H

4-H club members including ATQ-registered livestock, group entries, showmanship, and cloverbuds. ATQ number required for livestock entries.

4-H Categories
🐴

Equestrian & Gymkhana

Horse and pony entries including English, Western, and Gymkhana events. Emergency contact required. Gymkhana section checkboxes included.

Equestrian Classes

Registration Information

Exhibitor Numbers

Upon submission, you will receive a unique Havelock Fair exhibitor number (HF2026-####). Bring this number to the fairgrounds on competition day.

Multiple Entries

You may register up to 5 entries per submission on the General tab. For more entries, submit the form again with the same contact information.

Questions?

Contact our secretary Rommy Hernandez Tenorio at rommyh@hotmail.com or call (450) 826-1155 for registration assistance.

Need Help?

Our team is happy to assist with registration or answer any questions about competition classes and rules.
